Our Mission & Vision

Healing without side effects.

At Sri Tulasi Ayurvedic Hospital, our mission is to provide profound healing through authentic, traditional Ayurvedic methods, without the side effects often associated with modern medicine. We focus on diagnosing and treating the root cause of ailments, ensuring long-term wellness.

We believe in creating an environment that sparks tranquility and healing the moment you step onto our premises. We strive to become a sanctuary for those who seek reliable, safe, and holistic restorative care.

Infrastructure Highlight: Sri City Campus

Our premier facility, located in Sri City, is set on a lush 1-Acre Campus. We have carefully designed our wellness facilities to include:

  • Open Yoga Classes: To sync the mind, body, and breath in nature.
  • Sensory Pathways: Healing environments that engage your senses naturally through smell, touch, and sight.
  • State of the art Therapy Rooms: For authentic Panchakarma and specialized therapies.

NABH Certified & Cashless Enabled

We are proud to be an NABH Certified Ayurvedic Hospital. This prestigious certification ensures that we maintain the highest standards of safety, hygiene, and patient care matching the reliability of modern hospitals.

Furthermore, understanding the needs of our patients, we offer a seamless Cashless Insurance Facility. We have official tie-ups with 14 major insurance companies, ensuring that your journey to wellness is not hindered by administrative or financial stress. Focus entirely on your healing while our dedicated Help Desk handles the rest.
