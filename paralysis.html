Ayurvedic Approach to Paralysis (Pakshaghata)

In Ayurveda, paralysis is termed 'Pakshaghata' and is considered a Vata vyadhi (disease caused by Vata dosha vitiation). Our treatment focuses on clearing blockages in energy channels, nourishing the nervous system, and restoring muscle tone and joint mobility.

Benefits

  • • Improves muscle control
  • • Enhances joint mobility
  • • Strengthens nervous system

Duration

Typically requires 21 to 28 days of intense inpatient treatment.

Suitability

Patients recovering from stroke, facial nerve paralysis, or hemiplegia.
